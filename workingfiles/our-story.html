MISSION & VISION
WAKIM ZEIDAN

The original founder of Nuqat. With over eighteen years of experience in the advertisement field, Wakim (better know as Wakpot) founded Nuqat to revamp the stagnant creative industry in the Middle East. A graduate from the American University of Beirut in Graphic Design, Wakim is passionate about creative copy and conceptual thinking. He is currently the founder and creative director of Wakpot creative house, based in Beirut and Kuwait.
SARA AL NAFISI

The creative mastermind behind the Nuqat brand. With a passion for visual creativity Sara provides an original curation to the artistic talent that participates in all events. She is also the partner of Raw Design, being the secret ingredient to the success of the studio due to her artistic background and creative eye.. With a bachelors of Science in Graphic Design from the American University of Sharjah, she combines the creative aspect of design with the functional, resulting a unique end product.
HUSSA AL HUMAIDHI

One of the founders of Nuqat, Hussa is currently design strategist and partner of Raw Design Studio. With a bachelors of Science in Graphic Design from the American University of Beirut, Hussa is the strategist in the group. She approaches design as an attempt for complete communication from all its angles. Her passion for social development and creative cultivation is a continues mantra that she brings to the Nuqat table.
DANA AL HILAL

The booster of Nuqat, Dana Al Hilal is one of the founders with a great vision and large appetite for creativity. Dana is currently the PR executive of Way Communications,Kuwait. With over eightyears of experience in the PR world, she has organized many local and international conferences. Dana has boosted Nuqat from a modest idea to one of the largest conferences in Kuwait. Dana also brings a creative eye to the table, being an artist, she adds a passionate zest to the organisation.
 

our ambassadors
TALA SALEH

Nuqat’s very own creative writer and contributor, Tala Saleh is better known as the author of a book that addresses political graffiti as communication in Lebanon entitled “Marking Beirut”. A graduate from the American University of Beirut, she recently attained her masters in branding and identity from the London College of Communication. Tala is currently freelancing as a brand designer. Entering Nuqat as a speaker in 2010, Tala decided to join the team and since has been providing added value with her creative vision and energetic spirit.
NOOF KHONJI

The Nuqat communication consultant. With her experience in the marketing field Noof has always been interested in strategic thought and conceptual implementation. With this in mind Noof embarked on a two year journey at Central Saint Martins in the innovation world, where she learned how to take strategic thoughts and create tangible incremental solutions for the industry. Noof has helped provide a direction to Nuqat’s growth pattern, and is providing a blue print for the Nuqat Brand and its future endeavours.
BUSHRA BADRI

Bushra Badri is a Dubai-based graphic designer holding a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication from the American University of Sharjah. After a brief stint with Traffic, she practiced design as a freelancer. Most notably with Al Manakh, Art Dubai and the DIFC. She was also a delegate and a speaker for the Sheikha Manal art exchange program. She recently co-found UAEchronicled, a design line that uses cartography as a medium to study and re-visualize the country’s urban sprawls. Bushra is primarily interested in and focused on documenting, studying, and developing a visual identity for Gulf cities.
